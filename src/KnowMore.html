Introduction My name is Nirajan Joshi. I work as a software developer, helping businesses present themselves professionally online and make it easier for customers to reach and trust them. The problem Today, most customers search online before choosing a service. If a business is not clearly visible or does not look trustworthy online, customers usually choose someone else—even if the service is good. The solution A website works like a digital office for your business. It shows who you are, what services you provide, where you are located, and how customers can contact or book your service—without needing repeated explanations. Why a website helps your business Builds trust with new customers Makes your business look modern and professional Saves time—customers get information without calling or messaging Easy to share like a digital visiting card Helps customers find you on the internet Main features I provide One responsive website that works on mobile and computer A booking or inquiry form where messages come directly to you A WhatsApp button so customers can contact you instantly Google Maps link showing your exact location Image gallery to show your business or services About Us section to explain your story, staff, and values How booking works (simple explanation) A customer can see your website, book the service online, and you receive the message of the service that the customer wants immediately. When the customer arrives, payment and access can be handled directly at your counter. Your responsibility & my responsibility As a developer, my responsibility is to design a website that is clean, trustworthy, and easy to use. The business’s responsibility is to provide correct and honest information so customers feel safe and confident.
